How to play/

1Ape in. Your $1,000 rides a live market. The orange line climbs - and so does your cash-out value.
2Cash out before it rugs. Tap to bank your gains. The higher you ride, the bigger the number - and the likelier it all vanishes in an instant.
3Don't become the exit liquidity. Every run rugs in the end. Taking profit is the only skill.
About leverage (2x-10x): you borrow from the house at an absurd rate that compounds every second. You can cash out in profit and still owe money. That's the lesson, not a bug.
Controls: tap Ape in to start, tap again to cash out. On desktop, Space works too - and you can tap the chart to sell fast.
